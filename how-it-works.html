How It Works

For Donors

  1. Post Items: List items you wish to donate.
  2. Schedule Pickup: Set your preferred pickup times.
  3. Track Delivery: Receive updates on your donation.

For Recipients

  1. Browse Listings: Find items you need.
  2. Request Items: Submit a request.
  3. Receive Delivery: Get items delivered to you.

For Couriers

  1. Sign Up: Register as a courier.
  2. Accept Deliveries: Choose deliveries.
  3. Earn Money: Get paid per delivery plus tips.
